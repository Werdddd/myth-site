About Creation Myth

A creation myth is a traditional story or legend that explains how the world, human beings, and life came into existence. These myths are part of a culture's belief system and often reflect its values, worldview, and understanding of the universe. Typically passed down through generations, creation myths involve gods, supernatural beings, or powerful forces and are used to answer fundamental questions about the origin of life and the cosmos.
